## 🏆 Honors & Recognitions

Founded in 1902, Nanchang High School is one of the first batches of outstanding key high schools in Jiangxi Province. Guided by our motto **"Dare to Pioneer" (敢为天下先)**, the **Nanchang High School AI** club consistently dominates in regional and national hackathons and robotics competitions, maintaining formidable competitiveness in the tech arena.

<details>
  <summary><b>🥇 National Level Excellence</b></summary>
  <br>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Ministry of Education:</b> AI Education Base for Primary and Secondary Schools</li>
    <li><b>Ministry of Education:</b> First Batch of National Science Education Experimental Schools</li>
    <li>National Characteristic Unit for Youth AI Activities</li>
    <li>National Advanced Unit in Youth Science and Technology Innovation</li>
    <li>National "Xiaoping Science and Technology Innovation Laboratory" Construction School</li>
